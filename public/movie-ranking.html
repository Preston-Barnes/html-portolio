My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Interstellar

This is a great Sci-fi movie that invokes my love of the stars.

Jurassic Park

As child, I dreamed of discovering the bones of long, lost creatures thanks to this movie.

Godzilla vs Biollante

As an avid Godzilla fan, this movie embodies the destructive power of Godzilla as a force of nature not to be triffled with by mankind.
